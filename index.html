Overview

We introduce a CAER benchmark consisting of more than 13,000 videos

This benchmark contains more than 13,000 annotated videos.
You can use CAER benchmark to train deep convolution neural networks for emotion recognition.
The videos are annotated with an extended list of 7 emotion categories.

Download

Download CAER (available after July 23, 2019)

This benchmark contains more than 13K annotated dynamic videos.

Citation

Researcher shall use CAER benchmark only for non-commercial research and educational purposes.

If you use this dataset in your research, please cite this publication:

J. Lee, S. Kim, S. Kim, J. Park, and K. Sohn, "Context-Aware Emotion Recognition Networks,"
International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV), 2019.
